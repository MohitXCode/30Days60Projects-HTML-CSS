Ratan Naval Tata

1937 – 2024

Visionary Industrialist & Philanthropist

Ratan Naval Tata (28 December 1937 – 9 October 2024) was an Indian industrialist and philanthropist. He served as the chairman of Tata Group and Tata Sons from 1991 to 2012 and held the position of interim chairman from October 2016 to February 2017. Under his leadership, the Tata Group expanded globally, acquiring companies like Tetley, Jaguar Land Rover, and Corus. He was also known for his philanthropic efforts and investments in various startups. Ratan Tata passed away on October 9, 2024, at the age of 86.
